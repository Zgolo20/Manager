Module 01

🌡 Brooder Temperature Control

Reduce heat by ~3°C per week as chicks develop feathers. Watch for huddling (too cold) or panting/wing-spreading (too hot).

⚡ After Week 4: Wean off heat gradually over 2–3 days. Ensure night temps don't fall below 18°C.

Module 06

🔬 Feed Nutritional Value Guide

Feed is the biggest cost and the key to health. Use commercial feeds matched exactly to age and purpose.

💰 Cost & Management Tips

Buy in Bulk

Purchase 50–100 kg lots. Store in sealed containers away from moisture and rodents.

Transition Gradually

80/20 → 60/40 → 50/50 → 20/80 → 100% new feed over 5 days. Prevents digestive upset and feed rejection.

Calcium for Layers

Offer crushed oyster shell or limestone grit free-choice from Week 18. Thin shells = calcium deficiency.
